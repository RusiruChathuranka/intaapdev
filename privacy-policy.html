Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as “Policy”) explains how the IT company collects, uses, and discloses personal information in connection with the Services.

The IT company is committed to protecting the privacy of its clients and users.

Personal Information Collected

The IT company may collect the following personal information from clients and users:

  • Name
  • Contact information (e.g. email, phone number)
  • Payment information (e.g. credit card information)
  • Any other information that the client or user voluntarily provides
Use of Personal Information

The IT company may use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Services
  • To process payments
  • To communicate with the client or user

  • Disclosure of Personal Information

The IT company will not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

Despite these measures, the IT company cannot guarantee the security of personal information and assumes no liability for unauthorized access or use.

Data Retention

The IT company will retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Access and Correction

The client or user may request access to their personal information by contacting the IT company.

The IT company will take reasonable steps to correct any personal information that is inaccurate.

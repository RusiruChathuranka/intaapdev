Web and Mobility Solution Architect

Design, Aaency

A new career journey awaits with INTAAP

We are in search of dedicated and passionate team players who are excited about the opportunity to shape their career and bring their enthusiasm to drive change and progress. The Solution Architect will report to the Digital Engagement Product Line Lead and be responsible for designing and architecting digital websites, portals, and mobile apps for our company's commercial sales and marketing group. This role serves as the global technical authority for the Digital Engagement product line.

As a digital engagement expert, you will work closely with marketing leads, data analysts, and other members of the Business Technology organization to deliver business value. The Solution Architect should have experience in developing and maintaining websites and functionality using Web Content Management solutions such as WordPress (multisite, VIP Go). This role is responsible for guiding and designing back-end integrations and front-end web and mobile capabilities, including the development of Headless CMS architecture. The Solution Architect will also assist in managing business partners, agencies, and global support teams, as well as identifying opportunities to improve services and contributing to design and coding best practices.

The primary responsibilities include:
  • Meeting with clients to discuss website and mobile app design and function
  • Architecting high-performance and high-availability web and mobile applications
  • Managing all technical aspects of web Content Management Systems (CMS) and mobile development
  • Providing estimates for technical work effort for backlog items
  • Leading proof of concept/prototype efforts
  • Communicating at a high level with business owners, agency partners, and Business Technology teams
  • Managing and juggling multiple projects at once, working cross-functionally across business and Business Technology teams
  • Keeping up-to-date with the latest developments in online digital and mobile technologies and features to drive innovation and use on the platform
Minimum education requirements :
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent.
Experience & Skills
  • The ideal candidate should have 5+ years of experience in Web and Mobile Development, 3+ years in a Web CMS environment, in-depth knowledge of WordPress (multisite, setup and configuration, parent/child themes, plugins, Gutenberg, roles and permissions, etc.).
  • Experience with Adobe Experience Cloud, SiteCore or similar will be considered
  • Experience in platform agnostic mobile application development such as React Native/Xamarin and mobile web development is also required.
  • Additionally, proficiency in Hypertext Preprocessor (PHP), Hypertext Markup Language Version 5 (HTML5), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), JavaScript frameworks such as jQuery, React, or similar, SQL Server, Oracle and MySQL databases, CSS preprocessors, Git version control, and Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) tools is necessary.
  • Understanding design, User Interface (UI), and User Experience (UX) concepts and ability to translate them to technical implementations is desired.
  • Practical understanding of web analytics, website performance optimization and agile teams is also required.
